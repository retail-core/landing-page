Privacy policy

At Jarabiz, we understand that your business data is sensitive. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the platform properly:

  • Business details (store name, products, prices)
  • Sales and inventory records
  • Staff accounts and assigned roles
  • Contact details such as email address and phone number

How we use your information

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Record sales and track inventory
  • Generate reports and business insights
  • Manage staff access and activity
  • Improve system performance and reliability

We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

Who can access your data

  • You control who has access within your business
  • Staff only see what you allow them to see
  • Jarabiz staff do not access your data unless you request support

Data security

We take data protection seriously and use industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Secure servers
  • Encrypted connections
  • Restricted internal access

    • Your rights

    • Correct or delete your information.

    Changes to this policy

    If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you clearly. No hidden updates.
